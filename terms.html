Terms & Recommendations

Terms of Use (summary)

This page summarizes key terms and recommendations for using ResiCare demo system. In a real product you must include full legal terms, coverage limits, claim procedures, refund policies and privacy statements.

  • Coverage limits and exclusions will be described in the policy document.
  • Claims must be submitted with supporting evidence (photos, documents) as required.
  • Automatic decisions for minor claims are a demo and subject to human review.

Recommendations for users

  1. Keep photos and receipts of important items.
  2. Report incidents as soon as possible and follow the claim process.
  3. Check plan limits and exclusions before subscribing.
